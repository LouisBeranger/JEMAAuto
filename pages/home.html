Driving Beyond Boundaries,
Beyond Roads

In the fast lane of automotive trade, JEMA AUTO doesn't just navigate roads; it transcends boundaries and redefines the automotive experience. Born from the vision of Jema Africa Limited, JEMA AUTO is not merely a distributor; it's a digital platform revolutionizing the way vehicles and machinery reach new destinations.

A Journey Redefined

Established over nine years ago, JEMA AUTO has evolved from a traditional importer of used vehicles and machinery to a digital platform that expands horizons and possibilities. Our journey isn't just about covering distances; it's about breaking barriers and offering a spectrum of options beyond imagination.

Expanding Choices,
Empowering Decisions

In the era of digital transformation, JEMA AUTO has shifted gears to offer a dynamic digital platform. More than just a virtual showroom, our platform provides clients with a myriad of options, empowering them to make informed decisions that align with their unique preferences and needs.

Diverse Fleet,
One Platform

From Japanese imports to European elegance and Australian robustness, JEMA AUTO's digital platform hosts a diverse fleet. Catering to clients across Tanzania and other African countries like Zambia, Kenya, and Uganda, we bring a world of automotive choices to your fingertips.
